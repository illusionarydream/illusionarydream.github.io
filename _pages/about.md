I am Yuhao Quan, a final-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). Currently, I am a research intern at the [Digital Art Laboratory](http://dalab.se.sjtu.edu.cn/www/home/), advised by [Prof. Xubo Yang](http://dalab.se.sjtu.edu.cn/www/home/?page_id=143). 
I also work closely with [Prof. Wei Li](https://lwkobe.github.io/) at the [John Hopcroft Center (JHC)](https://jhc.sjtu.edu.cn/) for Computer Science.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics, robotics, differentiable physical simulation, and neuro-physical hybrid modeling.
