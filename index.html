illusionary@sjtu.edu.cn

Shanghai, China

I am Yuhao Quan, a final-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). Currently, I am a research intern at the Digital Art Laboratory, advised by Prof. Xubo Yang. I also work closely with Prof. Wei Li at the John Hopcroft Center (JHC) for Computer Science.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics, robotics, differentiable physical simulation, and neuro-physical hybrid modeling.
